Okra Soup Recipe

This is the recipe of one of the Nigerian most loved delicacies, the Okra Soup

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb Okra half minced and half sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • Smoked Turkey wings
  • 1 Small onion minced
  • 1 habanero pepper
  • 3 Tbsp crayfish
  • 1/2 lb Spinach
  • 1 lb shrimps

Steps

  1. Boil the Smoked Turkey wings with salt, seasoning powder and minced Onions. Cook till tender.
  2. While the meat is boiling, mince half of the onions in a food processor or a chopper and slice the other half. Set aside
  3. Boil the Smoked Turkey wings with salt, seasoning powder and minced Onions. Cook till tender.
  4. While the meat is boiling, mince half of the onions in a food processor or a chopper and slice the other half. Set aside
  5. Boil the Smoked Turkey wings with salt, seasoning powder and minced Onions. Cook till tender.
  6. While the meat is boiling, mince half of the onions in a food processor or a chopper and slice the other half. Set aside
  7. Boil the Smoked Turkey wings with salt, seasoning powder and minced Onions. Cook till tender.
  8. While the meat is boiling, mince half of the onions in a food processor or a chopper and slice the other half. Set aside
